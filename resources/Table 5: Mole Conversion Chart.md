# Table 5: Mole Conversion Chart

## Overview

This chart is a reference guide for mole calculations, showing how to convert between mass, number of particles (atoms or molecules), and volume using the mole as a central unit. Mastery of these conversions is fundamental in stoichiometry and chemical calculations.

---

## Key Knowledge Points

### 1. The Mole as a Central Unit
- The mole links mass, number of particles, and volume in chemical calculations.

### 2. Conversion Relationships

#### Mass ↔ Moles
- **To convert mass to moles:**
  - \( \text{moles} = \frac{\text{mass (g)}}{\text{molar mass (g/mol)}} \)
- **To convert moles to mass:**
  - \( \text{mass (g)} = \text{moles} \times \text{molar mass (g/mol)} \)

#### Moles ↔ Number of Atoms or Molecules
- **To convert moles to particles:**
  - \( \text{particles} = \text{moles} \times 6.02 \times 10^{23} \)
- **To convert particles to moles:**
  - \( \text{moles} = \frac{\text{particles}}{6.02 \times 10^{23}} \)

#### Moles ↔ Volume (for gases at STP)
- **To convert moles to volume:**
  - At STP: \( \text{volume (L)} = \text{moles} \times 22.4 \)
  - At SATP: \( \text{volume (L)} = \text{moles} \times 24.8 \)
- **To convert volume to moles:**
  - At STP: \( \text{moles} = \frac{\text{volume (L)}}{22.4} \)
  - At SATP: \( \text{moles} = \frac{\text{volume (L)}}{24.8} \)

## Mole Conversion Summary Table

| Conversion                  | Formula                                                    |
|-----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| Mass → Moles                | moles = mass (g) / molar mass (g/mol)                      |
| Moles → Mass                | mass (g) = moles × molar mass (g/mol)                      |
| Moles → Particles           | particles = moles × 6.02 × 10²³                             |
| Particles → Moles           | moles = particles / 6.02 × 10²³                             |
| Moles → Volume (STP)        | volume (L) = moles × 22.4                                  |
| Volume → Moles (STP)        | moles = volume (L) / 22.4                                  |
| Moles → Volume (SATP)       | volume (L) = moles × 24.8                                  |
| Volume → Moles (SATP)       | moles = volume (L) / 24.8                                  |

## Applications
- **Stoichiometry:** Calculating reactant/product quantities in chemical reactions.
- **Gas Laws:** Relating moles and volume for gases under standard conditions.
- **Particle Counting:** Determining the number of atoms, molecules, or ions in a sample.
